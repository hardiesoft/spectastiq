Spectastiq

Interactive spectrogram renderer and audio player optimised for touch devices

View all examples

Color palettes

Spectastiq supports three different color palettes for rendering the spectrogram: viridis, magma, inferno and greyscale.

Viridis

Viridis is a color palette in the yellow, green and blue range.

<script type="module" src="/path/to/spectastiq.js"></script>
<spectastiq-viewer src="/path/to/audio/my-audio-file.mp3">
  <div slot="player-controls">
    <button>My custom button</button>
  </div>
</spectastiq-viewer>
